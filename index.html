About Me

The Analyst Behind the Numbers

Not just dashboards — I build the data culture that makes them matter.

I'm Yunus, a Senior BI Analyst based in Dhaka, currently leading analytics at Optimizely across a $200M+ ARR portfolio. I work with 10+ global teams to turn fragmented data into a single source of truth that execs actually rely on.

Before Optimizely, I built the analytics backbone at ShopUp (one of Bangladesh's largest B2B platforms), helped retain 20K+ users at AponTech through ML-driven insights, and started my journey optimizing SQL queries at DataWorks.

What drives me? I believe data should simplify decisions, not complicate them. Whether it's a Power BI dashboard for 400+ users or an ML model predicting churn, if it doesn't change how someone acts, it's just decoration.
