Case Study 01 · Sales · Power BI · Governance
Sales Sight 360
ShopUp (Mokam CPG) · B2B · $195M+ Annual GMV
The Problem
Six departments at ShopUp were each running their own sales reports from different data pulls. VP Sales, Supply Chain, and Finance were routinely disagreeing on GMV figures — sometimes by millions — in the same meeting. There was no single version of the truth, no data governance, and no unified view of the $195M+ in annual B2B transactions.
The Approach
I audited every existing report, mapped 8+ data sources, and ran a KPI alignment workshop with all department heads. I then built a governed Snowflake data layer with data contracts, before constructing Sales Sight 360 — a unified Power BI platform with a single star-schema model, consistent DAX measures, and role-level access per department. Delivered in 6 sprints over 10 weeks.

Case Study 02 · ML · Retention · Anomaly Detection
Churn Defence & Revenue Recovery
AponTech Ltd · Healthcare eCommerce · 100K+ Users
The Problem
AponTech was losing high-value users silently — with no early-warning system, churn was only discovered after revenue had already walked out the door. Simultaneously, $150K in annual revenue was leaking through transaction anomalies that nobody was monitoring. Customer segments were treated as one homogeneous group, leaving significant basket-size growth on the table.
The Approach
I ran k-means clustering to segment the 100K+ user base into 6 behavioural cohorts, each with a targeted promotional strategy. On top of that, I built an XGBoost churn model trained on 14 behavioural signals — giving the team a 2-week early-intervention window. Finally, I automated anomaly detection across all transactions using isolation forests, replacing the entire manual review process with real-time alerts.
