Case Study 01ShopUp (Mokam CPG) · B2B · $195M+ Annual GMV
Sales Sight 360
How I ended 6 months of cross-department metric conflict and built the single source of truth for $195M+ in B2B decisions.
01
The Business Problem
Six departments at ShopUp were each running their own sales reports from different data pulls. VP Sales, Supply Chain, and Finance were routinely disagreeing on GMV figures — sometimes by millions — in the same meeting. There was no governance, no single version of the truth.
The Technical Solution
Audited every report, mapped 8+ data sources, ran a KPI alignment workshop. Built a governed Snowflake data layer with data contracts before constructing Sales Sight 360 — a unified Power BI platform with star-schema model, consistent DAX measures, behavioral segmentation, and inventory forecasting. Delivered in 6 sprints over 10 weeks.
The Business Impact
150+ users adopted it as their daily operational system. 90% of metric discrepancies permanently eliminated. MoM churn reduced 12%. 45% of idle warehouse capacity recovered through inventory intelligence, unlocking 20% incremental sales from reactivated stock.

Case Study 02AponTech Ltd · Healthcare eCommerce · 100K+ Users
Customer Intelligence & Revenue Defence
How I built an early-warning system that retained 20K users, grew basket size 50%, and stopped $150K in annual leakage.
02
The Business Problem
AponTech was losing high-value users silently — churn was only discovered after revenue had already walked out the door. Simultaneously, $150K in annual revenue was leaking through transaction anomalies that nobody was monitoring. All 100K+ customers were treated identically.
The Technical Solution
Built customer intelligence segmentation across 6 behavioural cohorts, each with a targeted promotional strategy. Built a behavioral risk scoring model trained on 14 engagement signals — giving the team a 2-week intervention window before loss. Automated transaction monitoring across all activity.
The Business Impact
20,000+ high-value users retained with 2-week advance warning. Average basket grew 50% ($16 → $24) through targeted cohort promotions. $150K in annual revenue leakage permanently stopped. Full transaction monitoring automated — zero manual review required.
